This is the first post on AI Builder Blog. I'm starting this as a place to think out loud about the intersection of artificial intelligence, energy systems, software engineering, and what it means to be a builder right now.

## Why this blog

The pace of change in AI and adjacent fields is genuinely unprecedented. New models, new architectures, new paradigms for writing software — it's a lot to track. I want a place to slow down and actually think through what's happening, what matters, and what doesn't.

## What you'll find here

Long-form writing on:

- **AI systems** — how they work, how to build with them, where they're going
- **Energy technology** — the infrastructure questions that often get overlooked in tech discourse
- **Software craft** — how programming itself is changing in an AI-augmented world
- **Future of building** — what it means to create technology in 2026 and beyond

This is a place for ideas, not takes. Posts will be as long as they need to be.
